The Value Gap

The gap is invisible until it is expensive.

The same business can be worth dramatically more, or fail to sell at all, depending on how ready it is. Three things quietly suppress value, and all three take time to fix.

01 / Owner dependence

If it runs through you, buyers discount it.

When customers, decisions, and know-how live with the owner, a buyer is purchasing a job, not an asset. That is the single most common reason a deal is repriced or walks.

02 / Undocumented value

Clean financials and depth move the multiple.

Recurring revenue, documented processes, and a second tier of management are what buyers pay a premium for. Most owners have not built and proven them yet.

03 / No runway

Value is built in years, not the final quarter.

Meaningful multiple expansion takes 18 to 24 months of deliberate work. The owner who starts 90 days before a sale has already left the gap on the table.
